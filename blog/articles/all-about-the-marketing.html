What the heck is Marketing?

Many people think of marketing only as selling and advertising. But it's only the tip of the marketing iceberg.

Marketing is defined as:

“

A social and managerial process by which individuals and groups obtain what they need and want through creating and exchanging products and values with others.

”

Ok, is anything more clear now? I doubt it. You would be amazed at how many marketing definitions you can encounter while reading and studying. Like in some way they are trying to confuse you.

Seth Godin said:

“

I define marketing as anything we do that changes the culture for the better.

”

It's quite philosophical and untrue, but who I am? Just another fake guru kitty writing an online article.

I will tell you that marketing is putting business theory into practice. Doing actions which leads you to successful product or service sale and keeping your end customers satisfied.

So basically anything you need to do to provide the best results for your business.

You will run into dozens of famous fake gurus videos where they invite you for special opportunities promising 6 or 7 figures income. They will teach you something here and there, and keep you motivated, but the reality is that you will not hear about what marketing is, what business is or even what are the best ways for you to succeed.

And that is only valuable lesson from their side, which unfortunately 99 percent of their customers don't see.

So how to do real marketing?

  • Come up with your goal.
  • Understand your capabilities.
  • Find the easiest way to start getting the results.
  • Collect data, analyse, tweak and repeat until you see progress.
  • Keep doing it or change your business model if failing in the first case.

Ok, so you have a product or service. You need the target market you want to reach. You need to define the customers/user tribe you want to reach. Depending on these two you will decide on further steps.

So when someone comes to you offering how to grow on Instagram or learn how to SEO your website, he is inviting you to learn a certain skill. Before that, you need to realize what skills and tools you need or don't need at all depending on your business description.

Mistake number one

Learning all trends and tools available instead of focusing on actions necessary for your business.

If you want to be a marketer you need a set of actions which provides results, and not to chase trends and try to grasp everything.

Trends are changing, business models are changing, so a real marketer adapts to it when necessary.

How to make a fast start?

It's an easy decision. You need to crush your ego, but eventually in any case you need to do that if you want to become a successful and mature person. Become a copykitty. Become a purple cow if you can, but being a copykitty is ok for now.

Find a person or business already successful in the industry close to your niche. Analyse the business and all visible actions. Contact them and ask for advice. If you can, offer them money to share an example of their workflow.

Of course, you will not contact your direct competition, but someone who looks like. Starting with an organized set of actions is the best start for anything. Even learning new skills.

During that process, you can define your future needs, change tools and tactics, upgrade and reorganize.
